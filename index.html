How does it work?

  1. Tap on the state of your choice to highlight it.
  2. Tap again to deselect it.
  3. Search a state by choosing one of the five default options.
  4. Once chosen, all the previously highlighted states are deselected and only the currently chosen one is highlighted.
  5. Tapping on each state also shows a corresponding keyframe animation denoting its name.
